Farrah Abraham Nude Photo Leaked After Twitter Hacked

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

Anyone who has seen MTV’s Teen Mom knows that Farrah Abraham can be a pretty confrontational, illogical, crazy girl. But last night on Twitter, Farrah seemed to be having a bipolar moment—maybe a side effect from all the plastic surgery she’s had done? The young mom posted an angry rant of tweets, including, “The album I made sucks a** I don’t know what I was thinking. Thank God I autotuned it otherwise it would have been even more of a disaster.” We were thinking the same thing! Unfortunately, it was all the work of an anti-Farrah hacker.

The tweets started to hate on the Teen Mom franchise. (And honestly, they probably weren’t far from the truth claiming the series was scripted!) But the piece de resistance was a nude photo, allegedly of Farrah. It has since been deleted, but it showed a tall, tan woman in a compromising position. This isn’t the first time Farrah has made news—the young reality star was all too happy to show off her breast implants. She’s addicted to plastic surgery, and has gotten a nose job and chin implant to correct an “ugly profile.” We thought she looked fine before!

The hacker said what we’re all thinking in some of the tweets, like “My ignorance of other people and my selfishness is why I get treated the way I do,” and “MTV is a joke.” Farrah seems to have regained her Twitter account and has deleted all the tweets. Whether or not she’ll seek legal help to prosecute (like Scarlet Johansson and Mila Kunis did after their nude photos were leaked) is not yet known. No one from Farrah’s team has spoken about the hack. What do you think of Farrah? I say she should set down the microphone and TV scripts and focus on raising her super-cute daughter, Sophia!
