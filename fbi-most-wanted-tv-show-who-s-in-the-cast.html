The spin-off of “FBI” has finally arrived. Dick Wolf’s highly anticipated series, “FBI: Most Wanted” features a talented group of actors. Here’s a list of who’s in the “FBI: Most Wanted” cast.

Julian McMahon

Julian McMahon made his acting debut in a 1989 episode of the television series “Rafferty’s Rules,” titled “That Old Black Magic.” That same year, he appeared in an episode of the TV series “The Power, The Passion.” In 1990, McMahon landed a recurring role on the series “Home and Away,” in which he played the character Ben Lucini until 1991. His next recurring role was on the daytime drama “Another World,” in which he played Ian Rain. McMahon’s other acting roles include “Profiler,” “Charmed,” and ”Nip/Tuck.” He plays the character Jess LaCroix on “FBI: Most Wanted.”

Nathaniel Arcand

Nathaniel Arcand made his acting debut in a 1994 movie titled “Savage Land.” In 1996, he played David Kitikmeot in an episode of “Due South” titled “The Mask.” Arcand secured a recurring role in the television series “North of 60,” in which he played the character William MacNeil from 1994 to 1997. Another one of his recurring roles was in the series “Blackstone.” He played the role of Victory Merasty from 2009 to 2015. Arcand’s other acting roles include “Heartland,” “Bull,” and “Supernatural.” Arcand plays Clinton Skye on “FBI: Most Wanted.”

Keisha Castle-Hughes

Keisha Castle-Hughes made her acting debut in the 2002 film “Whale Rider.” She then appeared in the 2005 film “Star Wars: Episode III—Revenge of the Sith,” in which she played Queen of Naboo. In 2011, Castle-Hughes joined the cast of “The Almighty Johnsons.” She played the character Gaia until 2013. Her other acting roles include “Game of Thrones,” “The Walking Dead,” “Manhunt,” and “Roadies.” Castle-Hughes plays the character Hana Gibson on “FBI: Most Wanted.”    

Kellan Lutz

Kellan Lutz made his acting debut in a 2004 episode of “The Bold and the Beautiful.” The following year, he appeared in an episode of “CSI: NY” titled “Tri-Borough.” The following year, Lutz made his film debut in the movie “Stick It.” He secured a recurring role in the series “Generation Kill,” in which he played the character Jason Lilley. His other acting roles include “What Men Want,” “The Comeback,” “90210,” and the “Twilight” film series. Lutz plays Kenny Crosby in “FBI: Most Wanted.”

Roxy Sternberg

Roxy Sternberg made her acting debut in the 2012 film “Meeting Place.” After that, she appeared in an episode of the television series “Pini” titled “You Want to Look Like That Part 1.” In 2017, Sternberg played the character Elizabeth for seven episodes of the series “Emerald City.” Her other acting roles include “Law & Order: UK,” “Mars,” and “Absentia.” Sternberg plays Sheryll Barnes in “FBI: Most Wanted.”
