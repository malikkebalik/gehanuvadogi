What Happened to Beomhan? His K-Pop Journey Takes a Shocking Twist

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

Beomhan, a captivating rapper and dancer of Chinese descent is making waves in K-pop. Know about his skills, internet presence and recent professional developments such as his FM Entertainment contract status.

What are FM Entertainment’s Allegations Against Beomhan?

Beomhan was first connected to FM Entertainment, a significant figure in the K-Pop sector. The course of the K-Pop trainee has taken an unexpected turn, shocking the K-Pop scene. FM Entertainment has formally terminated Beomhan’s contract.

The K-Pop scene is in disbelief about the reasons for this choice. By ending Beomhan’s exclusivity agreement, FM Entertainment made an important announcement on September 6.

According to reports, this decision was mutual, but the company’s statement which was given in both Korean and English raised questions among K-pop fans.

FM Entertainment claims that Beomhan stressed out both workers and viewers by distributing false information during his live broadcasts.

The termination of Beomhan’s contract is yet another illustration of the intricate dynamics at work in the K-Pop industry, which is no stranger to disputes and difficulties.

The K-Pop world paid a lot of attention to these charges and worried for his well-being. Beomhan has had to deal with difficulties like cyberbullying, physical attacks, battles with self-harm and suicide attempts, all of which have complicated his work.

What did Beomhan Say After Termination?

Beomhan has amassed a following through his numerous live broadcasts on different apps over the past couple of years. The trainee gained a lot of notoriety by participating in pop-up events with Jay Chang.

Beomhan would be frank and upfront with viewers about everything throughout the live streaming including his mental health. He talked about attempting suicide, confessed to self-harm and complained about his allegedly subpar living situation and treatment by the organization.

After FM Entertainment broke the news of his layoff, some were relieved to see him leave the company while others worried about how he would feel.

A few hours later after the termination announcement, Beomhan posted two pictures on his Instagram page along with a message to his followers. He begged for continuous support for his objective after noting that he and his supporters likely experienced the same level of perplexity and dissatisfaction.
