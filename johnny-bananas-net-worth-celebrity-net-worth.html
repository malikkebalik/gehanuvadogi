What is Johnny Bananas' Net Worth?

Johnny Bananas is a reality television personality who has a net worth of $200 thousand. Johnny Bananas, also known as John Devenanzio, was a cast member on the 17th season of The Real World on MTV. He appeared on the season The Real World: Key West, and at the time, was a recent graduate of Pennsylvania State University. He went on to appear in multiple reality shows for MTV, including, The Real World/Road Rules Challenge: The Duel, The Real World/Road Rules Challenge: The Inferno 3, The Real World/Road Rules Challenge: The Gauntlet 3, The Real World/Road Rules Challenge: The Island, The Real World/Road Rules Challenge: The Ruins, The Real World/Road Rules Challenge: Cutthroat, The Real World/Road Rules Challenge: Rivals, The Real World/Road Rules Challenge: Battle of the Exes, The Real World/Road Rules Challenge: Rivals II, and more recently, The Challenge: Free Agents.

While on each of these challenges, Johnny was known to be hyper-competitive and incredibly tactical with his approach to win. He often ruffle a few feathers of both his opponents and his friends in order to win making him an integral cast member on every challenge shows. Johnny was also involved in a lawsuit against HBO after he sued them for defamation related to the name, "Johnny Bananas," as this was also the name of a character on Entourage, he eventually lost the suit.
