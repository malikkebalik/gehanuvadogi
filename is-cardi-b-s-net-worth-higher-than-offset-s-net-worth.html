Is Cardi B's Net Worth Higher Than Offset's Net Worth?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 2, 2024

Rapper Cardi B no doubt has an impressive net worth. And her husband, Offset, makes his money in the music industry, too. The two artists go back and forth in their relationship, with divorce reports first surfacing in September 2020.

Though the pair later called off their split, some fans think it’s only a matter of time until they face another scandal. If they do divorce, who comes out with the most money? And how will they divide their assets? Discover who has accumulated more wealth.

Cardi B and Offset’s relationship timeline

Offset and Cardi B tied the knot in 2017, though the public wasn’t aware of their marriage until months after their public “engagement.” The rapper gave birth to their first child together, Kulture Cephus, in 2018. They separated later that year, following confirmation that Offset had cheated on his wife. They reconciled shortly thereafter.

In Septmeber 2020, following years of ups and downs, Cardi B filed for divorce. A source told Us Weekly it was “a troubled time.” However, by Cardi B’s 28th birthday the next month, they had reconciled. In an Instagram Live, the “WAP” artist explained they simply “got married early,” calling their relationship “dysfunctional.”

What is Cardi B’s net worth?

Cardi B rose to prominence on the reality series Love & Hip Hop: New York in 2015. But her breakout year followed her TV debut came in 2017 when she signed a record deal with Atlantic Records. She released her single, “Bodak Yellow,” which debuted atop the Billboard Hot 100 and earned multiple Grammy nominations.

In addition to music, Cardi B delves into other areas of business and entertainment. She released a collection with Fashion Nova, a popular clothing retailer. She has also worked in partnership with Pepsi, Reebok, and MAC Cosmetics, appearing in advertisements for the brands. Cardi B is worth an estimated $40 million as of February 2022, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

In the years since, Cardi B has continued to dominate popular music. Her debut studio album, 2018’s Invasion of Privacy, became the first by a female solo artist to win the Best Rap Album Grammy. Cardi B has also taken her talents to the big screen, appearing in the 2019 film Hustlers.

Offset also has an impressive net worth

Offset is best known as a member of the hip-hop trio Migos. Migos broke out with singles “Versace” and “Bad and Boujee” in 2013 and 2016, respectively. The group’s two albums, Culture and Culture II, both reached No. 1 on the Billboard 200 and earned them two Grammy nominations.

In addition to his work with Migos, Offset has pursued a career as a solo artist. He released a collaborative album, Without Warning, with rapper 21 Savage and producer Metro Boomin, in 2017. Offset’s debut solo album, Father of 4, was released in 2019. He worked with Cardi B on the second single, “Clout,” which also earned a Grammy nod.

In regards to net worth, Offset falls behind his wife, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Offset is worth a reported $26 million, in line with his Migos collaborators Quavo and Takeoff. Though it appears the artist is worth less than his wife, if their finances are combined, it shouldn’t matter. However, in December 2021, Cardi B gave her husband $2 million — indicating they don’t share accounts.
