Aubrey Burchell (born on January 6, 2001) is a 21 years old aspiring singer from Pittsburgh. She has been singing since the age of 11. Aubrey also participated in American Idol in 2018 and was in the top 70 but could not make it through. In 2022, this beautiful redhead rose to fame after participating in season 17 of America’s Got Talent.

In the audition, Burchell sang “Call Out My Name.” Her performance was spot on, and she impressed all the judges. When it came for the judges to comment, Simon Cowell said, “Simon, the fact that you made that song, your version was genius. Absolute genius. Was every no perfect? No. It didn’t matter because I do believe that we’re just witnessing somebody’s career about to skyrocket.”

Sofia Vergara also found Aubrey’s performance awe-inspiring and said, “Aubrey, you are the perfect package. You look so cool. Your voice, the song that you picked, everything was perfect. I think we love you already. I think America is going to fall in love with you. I believe you have a chance to win this competition.

Aubrey Burchell is Autistic

Yes, this is true; the lady from Pennsylvania has diagnosable autism. She revealed it while talking to the judges before the audition.

She said, “I want Grammys. And most of all, especially as a recently diagnosed level one autistic, I struggled for a substantial portion of my life, and I want to show other young autistics suffering in silence that you can follow your passion. You can not fit in the box, do what you do, and eat at it.”


Aubrey Burchell Has a Loving Boyfriend

Burchell suffered greatly from her neurological developmental disability, but the music kept her spirit high. Besides music, a person always has her back; that person is her boyfriend, Brandon J. Bellante.

Through quick research, we found that they used to be best friends, but in 2018 their friendship turned into a romantic affair. Now they live together in a sweet home in Pittsburgh and lead a happy life. Bellante is a barber by profession and also has a shop named Comrades Barbershop in Irwin.

Burchell Has a Family Of Artists

On January 6, 2001, Aubrey Burchell (age 21) was born to her father, Russell, and mother, Heather, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. As far as we know, Burchell’s dad is an engineer and passionate about singing. He is the one who showed Aubrey the path of singing.

The AGT contestant is the eldest child at home as she has two younger sisters, Brenna and Cayla Burchell. Cayla does ski ballet and has won many competitions. So, in short, we can say that most of the members of the Burchell family are associated with some form of art.


Aubrey Burchell Works at Target

During auditing, when judges asked Aubrey if she earns money from singing or if she has a different job, her reply was, “Your girl works at target.” She added, “On my breaks, I scurry off to the bathroom and sit on the floor. I set up my camera, and I do some singing to keep myself sane.”

From time to time, Aubrey participates in different singing fests and competitions like Greensburg Music Fest. You might not know that she also participated in ABC’s American Idol in 2018. She possesses experience in live performances not limited to national anthems. According to our sources, Aubrey spent years learning singing from her vocal coach Keith Harker.

Some of you might know that our beautiful star now works as a coach too. She takes virtual classes and helps her students get better at singing.
