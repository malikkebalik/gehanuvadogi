Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

Yanmar Ex3200 Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers,  Attachments, Features & Pictures

Yanmar Ex3200 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Yanmar diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled engine, which delivers 32 hp (23.9 kW) power at a rated rpm of 2600.

Yanmar Ex3200 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Yanmar 1.6L 3-cyl diesel engine, 6.3 gal Fuel tank, 1,100 lbs Rear lift (at 24″/610mm), 2,550 lbs weight, 4×4 MFWD 4WD, 4.2 qts oil capacity and infinite (3-range) forward and reverse gears.

Yanmar Ex3200 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a mowing deck, front-end loader, and backhoe.
