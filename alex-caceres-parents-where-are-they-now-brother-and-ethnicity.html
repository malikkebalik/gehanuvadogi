Meet Alex Caceres Parents – Where Are They Now? 

Caceres, a prominent MMA fighter, was born in Miami, Florida on June 20, 1988. His parents, mainly his Father, played a negative role in shaping his fighting journey.

His biological Father was a drug dealer. He grew up in a less affluent neighborhood. Being a son of a drug dealer was not easy, as Alex had a tough upbringing.

The fighter revealed that he was molested by a rival gang when his father went to prison. The unpleasant moment and turmoil led his family to stay away and move to a safer location in a more affluent suburb.

Staying away from such matters, Caceres trained at Young Tigers Foundation. The tough fighter often smiled during competitions, claiming himself as inventive and creative.

Before his professional debut in 2008, he fought in Miami’s underground backyard street fighting organization. He was nicknamed “Bruce Leeroy,” influenced by the character in the film The Last Dragon.

Despite Caceres’s tough upbringing in a criminal family, he undoubtedly tackled all the harsh situations and focused on nurturing his talent and passion for the sport.

Alex Caceres Brother And Ethnicity

Caceres’s family comprises his parents and his brother, José Leonardo Cáceres Ovelar. Alex is of Cuban Dominican ethnicity, as his family is originally from Cuba.

His brother Jose was born on 5 June 1987. The elder brother Jose is known as the “The Fresh Prince of Kendall” in the MMA arena.

He was born in Miami but is of Cuban and Dominican roots.

Alex and Jose’s passion for fighting was kindled at a young age. While attending the Young Tigers Foundation, he watched his brother’s performances.

At the training center in Miami, he began actively participating in underground fighting, honing his skills and developing his love for the game.

Remarkably, the fighter was influenced by the documentary Forks Over Knives. Alex has cut out dairy and processed meat from his primary diet. However, he doesn’t present himself as a vegan.

The generous and responsible man has an interest in spirituality. After facing multiple challenges, he secured entry into the UFC in March 2011.
