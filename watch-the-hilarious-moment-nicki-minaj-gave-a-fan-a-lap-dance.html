Watch The Hilarious Moment Nicki Minaj Gave A Fan A Lap Dance

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 8, 2024

Don't ask, don't get.

As Nicki Minaj continues the UK leg of her 'The Pinkprint Tour,' she continues to bring fans on stage to soak up the energy alongside her. 

But whilst performing in Nottingham this week Nicki got more than she bargained for when the female fan she pulled up on to the stage asked for a lap dance from the 'Anaconda' rapper.

"Can I be really cheeky and ask for a lap dance?" said the girl, much to Nicki's shock.
