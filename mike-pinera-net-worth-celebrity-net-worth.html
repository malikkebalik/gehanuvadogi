What is Mike Pinera's Net Worth?

Mike Pinera is an American guitarist who has a net worth of $1 million. Mike Pinera was born in Tampa, Florida in September 1948. He started off with the group Blues Image and had a hit single with "Ride Captain Ride". Pinera joined the group Iron Butterfly and later started the band Ramatam. He then started the band New Cactus.

Pinera played lead guitar for Alice Cooper from 1980 to 1982 and now performs with the Classic Rock All Stars. Blues Image was the house band at Thee Image in Miami Beach and then signed with Atlantic Records. Pinera joined Iron Butterfly and played on their fourth album Metamorphosis. He released his debut studio album Isla in 1979 and then the album Forever in 1979.
