Is 1000LB Sisters Going to Renew for Season 5&amp;6?

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

As a result of their time spent on 1000 Lb Sisters, sisters Tammy and Amy Slaton gained widespread notoriety. Only two years have passed since their first TV debut. Their popularity has skyrocketed in a short amount of time. There has been no official word on whether or not the show would be renewed by the TV network.

Season 3 ended with an episode in January 2022. Since it has been a whole year since then, naturally viewers are excited for the premiere of the new season. TLC has ordered two more seasons of “1000 Lb Sisters,” according to a reliable source. Please read on for further information.

Since the show chronicled their efforts to lose weight, Tammy and Amy have become well-known figures. They’re both very busy people. While Tammy is in treatment to lose weight, her sister Amy is keeping quite busy with the arrival of twins. Despite this, TLC has not provided any details on renewing their travels for a new season. But according to The Sun, a trusted source close to Tammy Slaton has revealed some shocking details. The fifth season of the TV show starring the Slaton family is reportedly now in production.

However, Amy Slaton will not be a part of Season 5 of 1000 Lb Sisters. She’s “moving out of the spotlight” because the new season is all about Tammy Slaton. In an earlier interview, the younger sister threatened to leave the show unless her working hours were reduced. She had been feeling like a bad mother, which was terrible. As a result, her current top focus is being a present mother to her sons, Glenn and Gage.
