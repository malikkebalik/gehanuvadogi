Does Kim Kardashian Have Any Implants?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Entertainment

What's the truth about Kim Kardashian's body?

Published on April 22, 2019

2 min read

It’s something everyone asks, but few actually know: How much of Kim Kardashian’s body is fake? The Kardashians have a body type that is hard to attain on its own; they have massive rear ends and perfectly flat stomachs. But the question remains: Does Kim Kardashian have any implants?

The Kardashians have openly admitted to having personal trainers to keep them in shape

The Kardashians have not been shy about working with personal trainers. On their show, they’ve been seen on several occasions spending time with trainers and dedicating a few hours to intense workouts. When you’re famous, people critique everything, which might be why the sisters are always so focused on their bodies — and that includes Kim Kardashian, who has a long past full of modeling. Kardashian’s butt workout includes six moves that she does only twice per week, though it appears to be a lot more often than that. But since the legs and glutes need time to recover, it’s not something that should be done daily.

Kardashian once had an x-ray to prove her butt is real

Kardashian has always stood by the fact that her butt is completely real, but still, critics were not convinced. A few years back, Khloe Kardashian set the record straight by tweeting out a photo of Kim Kardashian standing next to her own x-ray. Kardashian got an x-ray to prove to persistent haters that her butt didn’t contain any implants or filler. “Hey dolls. The PROOF is in the X-ray. Kim’s a** is 100% real!!!” Kardashian’s sister tweeted. Kardashian had had enough of the constant rumors, so she decided to turn to science to show everyone she wasn’t lying about her assets.

Kardashian remains staunch that she has never received any kind of implants  

Kardashian has always remained very stiff about the fact that she’s never had implants of any kind; neither her butt nor her breasts are fake, according to the model. “I have not ever had plastic surgery,” Kardashian said back in 2008. “I have had a size C since I was 11 years old.” Old photos of Kardashian do prove that she’s always been more filled out than most women, and her breasts have likely only grown on their own since she had kids. The x-rays are probably the closest the public will get to Kardashian proving everything is real, though there will always still be some skeptics who say she faked the photo.  

Kardashian and her sisters have been called out for promoting weight loss products

Kardashian and her sisters have all been guilty of promoting fat-burning weight loss products on their Instagrams, and people have since called them out for it. Critics say the ladies aren’t truthful about how they get their bodies, which includes intense workouts and healthy meals. Kourtney Kardashian has since deleted one post promoting weight-loss tea, but the sisters have clapped back and said they’ve always been truthful about their workout regimens. Khloe Kardashian also noted that she does not have a personal chef.
