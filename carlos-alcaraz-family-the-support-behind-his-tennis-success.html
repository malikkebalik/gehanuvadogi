Carlos Alcaraz Family: The Support Behind His Tennis Success!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Carlos Alcaraz has been one of the best young tennis players ever. He has been great since his first tour, and there are signs that he will rule the Men’s circuit in the coming years. The 21-year-old has won four ATP titles and became the youngest person to win the ATP title since Kei Nishikori in Umag. Carlos Alcaraz has proven to be a natural successor to Rafael Nadal, a famous tennis player from Spain.

Even though Alcaraz is still young and has a long way to go, he has the potential to rule the tour as the King of Clay did. Alcaraz has shown what he can do by winning the US Open 2022 and making it to the Wimbledon final in 2023. But, like every other great tennis player, their parents greatly help them. Alcaraz’s parents were with him when he was a child, and now they watch him rule the world. In the article below, we will discuss the Carlos Alcaraz family.

Who Is Carlos Alcaraz Family?

Carlos Alcaraz is a rising tennis star with much to thank his folks for. They have always been there for him and helped guide him. Carlos Alcaraz Sr., a former professional tennis player and the head of the Real Sociedad Club de Campo de Murcia, was essential to his son’s early growth. From showing him the basics of the game when he was four to attending big tennis tournaments together, their relationship has helped Carlos Jr. get where he is today.

Carlos Alcaraz Father

Carlos Alcaraz is the son of Carlos Alcaraz González, also known as Carlos Alcaraz Sr. In the early 1990s, he was one of the top 40 tennis players in Spain and had a successful career as a pro. He also ran the Real Sociedad Club de Campo de Murcia as its head.

When Carlos was only four years old, he taught him how to play tennis and became his teacher and guide. Since then, he has attended tennis matches with Carlos, including the ATP 1000 Madrid Masters.

In 2018, Carlos Sr. signed his son up for Juan’s Sports Academy, where a former world number one player, Juan Carlos Ferrera, became his new teacher and helped him improve his skills. In an interview with Espana Diario, Alcaraz talked about his father and said, “He knows a lot about tennis.

Carlos Alcaraz Mother

Virginia Garfia Escandón is Carlos Alcaraz’s mother. She was in her 20s when she got married to Carlos Alcaraz Gonzalez. At the time, she was living in Spain.

Even though she dislikes sports, she has been there for her husband and son as they played tennis. Virginia has attended many events, including the US Open Championships in 2022, where her son won for the first time.

Does Carlos Alcaraz Have Any Siblings?

Alvaro, Sergio, and Jaime are the names of Carlos’s three brothers. Alvaro Alcaraz Garfia, his older brother, is an actor. Sergio and Jaime Alcaraz, his younger brothers, go to school.
