What Is the Birth Order of the Kardashian-Jenner Kids?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Contrary to popular belief, it’s not easy to keep up with the Kardashian-Jenners. They’re a pretty big family by most standards — and it only gets bigger as the years go on. But we’ve made it easy for you to stay updated. This is the birth order of the Kardashian-Jenner kids.

Mason Disick

Birth order: First. Mason is the oldest of the Kardashian-Jenner children.

Birthday: December 14, 2009.

Parents: Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick.

Current age: 9.

Penelope Disick

Birth order: Second.

Birthday: July 8, 2012.

Parents: Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick.

Current age: 7.

North West

Birth order: Third.

Birthday: June 15, 2013.

Parents: Kim Kardashian and Kanye West.

Current age: 6.

Reign Disick 

Birth order: Fourth.

Birthday: December 14, 2014.

Parents: Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick. Reign is their third and last child. Kardashian and Disick split in the summer of 2015 after photos emerged of Disick partying with several women, including his ex-girlfriend, Chloe Bartoli. However, the two remain friends and have a healthy co-parenting relationship.

Current age: 4.

Saint West

Birth order: Fifth.

Birthday: December 5, 2015.

Parents: Kim Kardashian and Kanye West.

Current age: 3.

Dream Kardashian

Birth order: Sixth.

Birthday: November 10, 2016.

Parents: Rob Kardashian and Blac Chyna. Dream is their only child. The pair ended its on-off relationship for good in July 2017 when a fight prompted Kardashian to release nude photos of Chyna online. They’ve had some ups and downs since but, overall, it appears that they try to stay cordial for the sake of Dream.

Current age: 2.

Chicago West

Birth order: Seventh.

Birthday: January 15, 2018.

Parents: Kim Kardashian and Kanye West.

Current age: 1.

Stormi Webster

Birth order: Eighth.

Birthday: February 1, 2018.

Parents: Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott (whose real name is Jacques Webster).

Current age: 1.

True Thompson

Birth order: Ninth.

Birthday: April 12, 2018.

Parents: Khloé Kardashian and Tristan Thompson. True is their only child. Kardashian and Thompson ended their nearly three-year relationship in 2019 after the NBA star got caught up in a series of cheating scandals, including one with Kylie Jenner’s BFF, Jordyn Woods. Drama aside, Thompson honored his ex-girlfriend on her birthday this year, calling her the “most beautiful” person he knows in an emotional Instagram post.

Current age: 1.

Psalm West

Birth order: Tenth.

Birthday: May 10, 2019. 

Parents: Kim Kardashian and Kanye West.

Current age: Two months.

There could be more on the way

If reports are accurate, at least one person in the family is ready to have another kid — Kylie Jenner. A source told People magazine in July 2019 that the 21-year-old is reportedly so “happy with her life” with her boyfriend and their 17-month-old daughter that she’s ready to expand their family.

“Kylie loves being a mom and can’t wait to give Stormi a sibling,” the insider said. “Everyone thinks Kylie will be pregnant with her second baby soon. They are trying.”

Does Kendall Jenner want kids?

Did you notice Kendall Jenner’s absence from this story? She’s currently the only member of her immediate family that doesn’t have kids. Speaking to E! News in May 2019, the supermodel said she goes “in and out of phases” when it comes to having children.

“I go in and out of phases,” she said of getting baby fever. “Some days, I’ll be there and I’ll be like, ‘[Oh my God], I think it’s happening. I think I have the fever.'”

“Most days, I’m like, ‘This is too much.’ It’s a lot to be able to play with them,” she continued in an apparent reference to her nieces and nephews. “I’ll just be like, ‘go to your mom.'”

She might not have kids yet, but you never know what’ll happen. So don’t count her out of this list just yet.
