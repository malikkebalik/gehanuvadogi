Meet Secrets & Sisterhood Queen Khadija Sozahdah

Khadija Sozahdah (age: 46, June 1, 1977) is of the 10th Sozahdah sisters who are making waves in the entertainment industry with the all-new Hulu show “Secrets & Sisterhood: The Sozahdahs.”

It is a reality show which revolves around Khadija and her sisters showcasing glimpses from their personal and professional life. According to sources, the Sozahdah came to the United States in the early 80s as Afghani immigrants.

The show’s main attraction is Shakur Sozahdah, a model who reigned hearts in the late 90s and early 2000s. If you crave dramatic reality shows packed with saucy and intense scenes, then you would definitely love “Secrets & Sisterhood: The Sozahdahs.”

Early Life and Journey to America

On June 1, 1977, Khadija Sozahdah (age 46) was born in Kabul, Afghanistan. Although very limited data is available, we learned that Khadija, with her parents and elder sisters, came to America at a very young age.

In America, the Sozahdah family settled in Riverside, California. Khadija and her sisters Shaista, Halimah, Rabya, Shakur Sozahdah, Muzlefa, Siddiqa Sozahdah, Nooreya, Hamida Sozahdah, and Jamila attended John W. North High School in Cali.

In childhood, Khadija was a bright student; her thirst for knowledge and eagerness to learn various subjects were fueled by the desire to make a difference in the world.

Khadija Sozahdah’s Husband: Qais Zafari

Yes, the third eldest, Sozahdah’s sister, is a happily married woman. Khadija’s husband is Qais Zafari, who is an attorney by profession.

According to sources, Qais is also the founder of Zafari Law Firm and has handled cases like Immigration, Bankruptcy, Debt, and Criminal Defense since 2007.

He is also an Afghan immigrant who came to America with his family after things went south in Afghanistan. Khadija and Qais also have beautiful kids, but they keep them away from camera attention.

Exploring Khadija Sozahdah’s Instagram Account

If you visit her Instagram handle (@theonlykhadija), you will learn that she created her IG account only a month before the release of “Secrets & Sisterhood: The Sozahdahs.”

It is believed that she does not like much modernization and is very loyal to her culture. Even in the Hulu show trailer, she made it clear that she is very religious and sometimes does not agree with her younger sisters’ views.

Khadija Sozahdah’s Profession and Career

Of all the sisters, Khadija is the one who does not share much about her professional life. She also does not maintain a LinkedIn account, so people do not know what this reality TV show star earns her bread from.

Despite less information, we learned that Khadija works as a Family Financial Planner. An FFP is an advisor/consultant who, keeping in mind the necessary expenses of a family, helps create a strategy to achieve long-term financial goals. In short, she guides her clients in managing their funds.
