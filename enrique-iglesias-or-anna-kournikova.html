Enrique Iglesias or Anna Kournikova?

Enrique Iglesias and Anna Kornikova have secreatly welcomed another child. Find out how many children they have now and a few more things about the private couple including who has a higher net worth.

Published on February 14, 2020

2 min read

Enrique Iglesias and Anna Kournikova just stunned the public with the announcement that they welcomed another child. This isn’t the first time the singing sensation and former tennis star surprised everyone with their baby news though and because they’re one of the most private celebrity couples on the planet fans always have questions about the pair.

Here are a few we can answer including how many children they have now and who has a higher net worth.

Iglesias and Kournikova just quietly welcomed another baby

On Feb. 13, they shared the happy news that Kournikova gave birth to a baby girl on Jan. 30, 2020.

The proud parents posted a few pictures on each of their Instagram accounts with the caption, “My Sunshine.”

Word of the new addition to their family was so surprising since fans didn’t know Kournikova was even pregnant, which was the same story when the pair welcomed their twins in December 2017.

After that announcement was made social media users flocked to Kourinkova’s account to see if there were any signs they missed, but there wasn’t much evidence that she was expecting. The only thing fans could point to was that Kournikova stopped posting full-body shots in the months leading up to the births of the twins.

This time around the retired tennis player posted a good number of throwback shots pre-pregnancy, so no one suspected anything. However, according to the magazine Hola!, the “Bailando” singer’s brother, Julio José, let the news slip in a recent interview when he said, “My brother already has three [children]. He is super happy with the baby and his music.”  

Because Iglesias and Kournikova always remain tight-lipped, it’s anyone’s guess if they want or will try for more kiddos in the future.

The “Be With You” artist did previously open up about how much he loves being a father.

“It feels incredible. It’s one of the best feelings in the world,” he gushed to British TV host Vick Hope. “I wake up in the morning and I’m like, ‘I’m a dad.’”

Who has the higher net worth?

Something fans do know about Iglesias and Kournikova is that they have been very successful in their careers. Iglesias is a recording artist and global superstar while Kornivkova played tennis and was one of the most sought-after female athletes in terms of endorsement deals for many years. So who actually has a higher net worth?

During her playing career, the Russian-born star won Grand Slam doubles titles in Australia in 1999 and 2002 with partner Martina Hingis. Kournikova was also ranked the No. 1 female player in the world a number of times. However, she had to retire early due to back injuries. Post-tennis, she was featured as a celebrity trainer on The Biggest Loser. These days, Kournikova has an estimated net worth of $60 million.

Iglesias, who is often referred to as The King of Latin Pop and The King of Dance, has recorded 10 studio albums, seven compilation albums, and released more than 70 singles. Today, his net worth is $100 million.
