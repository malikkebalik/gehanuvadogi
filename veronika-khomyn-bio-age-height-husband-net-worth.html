Early Life

Veronika Khomyn was born on 1st March 1990 and was brought up along with her other siblings, said to be sister, whose identity has not been given yet. She resided with her parents and other extended families in Ukraine. Veronika has completed her elementary school and high school education at Kyiv High School, Kyiv, where she studied along with her sister. Before completing her high school graduation, Veronika landed herself certain commercials and advertisement projects. At the age of 18, she started working as a model in Ukraine while she was still in high school.

According to a source, after completing her high school in Kyiv City Veronika moved to the US where she started gaining education in fashion design from the George Mason University in Fairfax, Virginia. While at the university, Veronika acquired her US citizenship and established her fashion and modeling career. At the same time (while pursuing her education at the university), she started dating another young personality, Sean McVay. However, her website states that Veronika received her bachelor’s degree in international business from George Mason University and later she received her master’s degree in global management from Arizona State University.
